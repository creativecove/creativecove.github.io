Commmon Exercises

The following are common exercises that we incorage our clients to do as part of theire daily exercise routiene.

Burpee
Burpees are great, full body exersize to increase your strength ansd endurince. Begin in a standing position, drop into a squiat and extend your hands foward, kick you feet back and then foward again quickly, then jump up from a squatted position.
Plank
Planks build your core strenght. To preform a plank, get in a push up position and rest your forearms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can.
Mountain Climber
Mountain Climbers are a good cardio exercise. Place your hands on the floor on a push up positon, then bring on n=kneed up to your chest and then switch as quickly as you can (as though you are climbing a mountain).
